Marketing & Public Relations Management Programme (MPMP)

Master the art of marketing and public relations to drive organizational success.

Programme Details

Overview

The MPMP brings an expansive understanding of the key theoretical and practical concepts in marketing management. The focus of the content reflects the changing marketing landscape and the tools required to excel in the field.

When organizational success is underpinned by acquiring and retaining customers, marketing is conceivably the most important commercial discipline. Hence the MPMP is curated to nourish participants with contemporary core concepts that can be applied to the marketplace.

The course will wrap with a summary of the key learning points, followed by an action planning exercise with a view to apply the acquired knowledge and skills immediately upon participants’ return to work. Post-course support is also available in relation to the implementation of a participant’s action plan, for up to six (6) months following course completion.

Programme Content

  • Defining Marketing Objectives
  • Elements of a Marketing Plan
  • Marketing Models and Case Studies
  • Market Research, Analytics, and Insight
  • Understanding Primary and Secondary Research
  • Technological Considerations in Research
  • Quantitative and Qualitative Research
  • Trends Analysis and Market Overview
  • Demographics, Consumer Psychology, and Psychographics
  • Creating Value in a Business Proposition
  • Specifying Values and Unique Selling Propositions
  • Market Planning – Segmentation, Targeting, Positioning
  • Marketing Communications, Branding, and Design
  • Managing a Marketing Budget
  • Delivering Return on Investment (RoI)

Target Participants

This course is suitable for:

  • Media Planners and Marketing Executives
  • Account Managers and Brand Managers
  • Marketing Managers and Business Development Executives
  • Senior Managers and Account Directors
  • Corporate Communications Teams
  • Data Scientists and Marketing Directors
  • Design Directors, Business Leaders, and Entrepreneurs

Learning Outcomes

Upon completion of this course, participants will be able to:

  • Develop a strategic marketing plan
  • Utilize analytical tools to refine the organization's marketing strategy and effectiveness
  • Guide the organizational marketing strategy focused on return on investment
  • Determine the best marketing channels and methodologies for campaigns
  • Harness advanced marketing management principles
  • Align marketing and branding strategies for Strategic Business Units (SBUs)
  • Troubleshoot underperforming aspects and revolutionize marketing management
  • Implement an Action Plan using the knowledge and skills acquired
