About Us

Ghana International Business School (GIBS) is a registered knowledge broker; management training, innovation and consulting services provider for Private, Public and Civil Society Organisations (CSOs).

Established in the year 2006, GIBS has distinguished itself as provider of high impact strategic executive education programmes with deep African insights, curated to prepare participants and client organisations for global business success.

The advent of the African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA); requires deliberate efforts to develop the capacities of owner-managers and key executives of Micro, Small and Medium-sized Enterprises (MSMEs) in particular, to navigate the complex business terrain, to access the opportunities that beckons.

This is an imperative that cannot be left to chance. GIBS stands in the best place to meet this critical need. Following decades of experience working with African enterprises in leadership management and administration; we have developed the capacity to assess client needs and co-create the most innovative and rewarding solutions for client only; and or client consultant co-execution.

Goal and Objectives

Goal

Our goal is to adequately equip owner/managers and executives of African enterprises to become highly competent in a wide range of global business subject areas and themes to enable them to, effectively and efficiently access intra-African trade opportunities as dominant players, and operate in the global business arena competitively.

Objectives

Anchored on the philosophy that good corporate governance, exemplified in leadership and innovation that permeates management and administrative practices are key success factors for contemporary international business, our objectives are:

  • To equip leaders and managers of African enterprises with the relevant know-how to dominate intra-African trade and tap into its endless opportunities to become global giants of enterprise.
  • To provide the vehicle (Think Tank) for the intellectual calibration of strategic business subjects and themes that generate synergy to propel African enterprises to make global business impact for Africa’s development.

Mission & Vision

Mission

Continuously provide innovative practice based, world-class knowledge, skills and appropriate attitudes for leaders of Africa’s enterprises towards international business success.

Vision

Become Africa’s leading Centre of Excellence in Leadership, Corporate Governance; Innovation, Information Technology, Management and Administration.

PARTNERSHIPS

GIBS in partnership with the London Business Training & Consulting (LBTC), offers 15 regular programmes contemporaneously in the two great capital cities of London and Accra. The partnership dubbed “One Content Dual Locations” offers our participants the options to be trained in Accra or London.
