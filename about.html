About GIBS

Ghana International Business School (GIBS) is a premier institution focused on developing professionals with strong business acumen, ethical grounding, and practical knowledge. We offer a variety of programs in business, finance, IT, and management.

Our programs are tailored to meet the current demands of the job market and are delivered by experienced lecturers and industry professionals.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide industry-relevant education and training that empowers learners to make impactful contributions in business and society.

Vision: To be the leading provider of practical and professional business education in Africa.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: We uphold ethical standards in all we do.
  • Innovation: We promote creativity and problem-solving.
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in education.
  • Leadership: We prepare students to lead with impact.

Accreditation & Affiliations

GIBS is registered and accredited by the Ghana Education Service and affiliated with both local and international institutions, ensuring the global relevance and recognition of our certifications.
