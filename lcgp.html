Leadership & Corporate Governance Programme (LCGP)

Empowering leaders with the tools to excel in corporate governance.

Programme Details

Overview

Corporate Governance is about what company boards do and how they are expected to do them by prescribed codes, regulations, principles, best practices, and legislation. The LCGP examines ‘what good looks like’ for members of a board when carrying out their duties to arrive at good corporate governance.

It wraps on a summary of the key learning points, followed by an action planning exercise with a view to apply the acquired knowledge and skills immediately upon participants’ return to their office. Post-programme support is also available in relation to the implementation of a participant’s action plan; up to six (6) months following programme completion.

Programme Content

  • Corporate Governance Fundamentals and Theories
  • Corporate Governance Codes/Ghana’s Companies Act 2019, Act 992
  • Role of the Board
  • Corporate Purpose and Strategy
  • Corporate Risk, Corporate Culture and Ethics
  • Balancing the Interests of Shareholders and Stakeholders
  • Board Composition and Structures
  • Board Committees
  • Creating Board Diversity
  • Employee Representation on the Board
  • Effective Board of Directors
  • Directors’ Legal Duties per Act 992
  • Evaluating Board Performance
  • Leadership, Independence and Governance
  • How to Manage Effective Board Meetings, Meeting Agenda, Minutes, Schedules, etc.

Target Participants

This course is suitable for:

  • Non-Executive Directors wanting to understand their role
  • Current Board Chairs and Members wishing to refresh their knowledge and skills
  • Chief Executives, Company Secretaries, Professionals, Practitioners, and Managers in Corporate Investment
  • Leaders in State-Owned Enterprises, Public, Voluntary, and Non-profit Organisations

Learning Outcomes

Upon completion of this course, participants will be able to:

  • Understand the fundamental principles of corporate governance
  • Describe the role of the Board of Directors
  • Explain how boards are structured and operate, including working through Board Committees
  • Recognise the benefits of board member diversity, including gender, ethnic, and functional diversity
  • Describe the legal duties of directors per the Companies Act 2019, Act 992
  • Review how to run an effective board meeting
  • Explain the roles and skills needed for key appointments to the board
  • Implement a performance measurement structure for individual directors and the board as a whole
  • Implement an Action Plan at your workplace using the knowledge and skills acquired through the course
