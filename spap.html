Secretaries & Personal Assistants Programme (SPAP)

Empowering Secretaries and PAs to excel in their roles with C-Suite Executives.

Programme Details

Overview

The value of a good Secretary or PA cannot be over-emphasised. The course is designed to give the modern Secretary and PA the tools required to excel at working with C-Suite Executives.

The course will wrap with a summary of the key learning points, followed by an action planning exercise with a view to apply the acquired knowledge and skills immediately upon participant’s return to work. Post-course support is also available in relation to the implementation of participants’ action plans, up to six (6) months following course completion.

Programme Content

  • Time Management and Productivity Techniques
  • Organizing Meetings and Events Planning
  • Office IT and Records Management
  • Minute-taking Essentials
  • Presentation Skills Excellence
  • Written Communication
  • Giving and Receiving Constructive Feedback
  • Effective Video Communication

Target Audience

This course is suitable for:

  • Senior Secretaries
  • Executive Assistants
  • Personal Assistants
  • Senior Administrators
  • Secretaries and Assistants looking to take their career to the next level

Learning Outcomes

Upon completion of this course, you will be able to:

  • Develop the most efficient skills and techniques in time management
  • Appreciate the key elements for organizing meetings and events
  • Understand the role of good records management in organizations
  • Develop your presentation skills to improve your performance
  • Understand the key elements for producing excellent written communications
  • Appreciate the importance of being able to give inspiring feedback
  • Implement an Action Plan at your workplace using the knowledge and skills acquired through the course
