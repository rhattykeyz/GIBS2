Admissions

Follow these steps to apply for any of our diploma, short course, or professional programmes.

Download Application Form
1. Review Entry Requirements

Each programme has specific prerequisites. Generally, applicants need a secondary school certificate or its equivalent. For executive-level courses, relevant work experience is required.

2. Choose Your Programme

Browse our Programmes page and select the course that matches your career goals.

3. Complete Application Form

Download and fill out the application form, then attach:

  • Certified copies of academic certificates
  • Curriculum Vitae (CV)
  • Proof of payment of application fee
4. Submit Documents

Send your application package to our Admissions Office by email or in person at:

Ghana International Business School (GIBS)
No. 304/14 Odotei Tsui Close, Dzorwulu–Accra, Ghana
Email: gibsaccra@gmail.com
Phone: +233 243 588 422
5. Application Review & Offer

Our admissions team will review your documents and notify you of our decision within 7–10 business days.

6. Registration & Payment

Upon acceptance, complete registration and pay tuition fees to secure your place. Detailed payment instructions will be provided in your offer letter.
