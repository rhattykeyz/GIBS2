Fund Raising and Financial Management for Business

Date(s): TBA

Course Description

With a target audience of SMEs and entrepreneurs, this course gives participants an overview of the processes involved in raising various kinds of funds for their activities. It begins by looking at a few techniques to evaluate financial need. Then, it explores the differences between equity, grants and debt (both long and short-term), and details the various capital providers from which SMEs might raise funds. Finally, it concludes with a detailed examination of the process of securing funds, due diligence, and the post-funding relationship that you might want to build with your capital provider.

Course Structure

  • Module 1: Raising Funds for Your Business
  • Module 2: Choosing the Right Capital Provider
  • Module 3: Securing Funds
  • Case Study Exercise

Learning Outcomes

After completing this course, participants will be able to:

  • Step by step raise funds for your business!

Course Certification

Participants who meet the course requirements will receive a Certificate of Participation and Completion.
